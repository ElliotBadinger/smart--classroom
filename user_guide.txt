### Taking Images

To take images, follow these steps:

1. Click on the "Take Images" button.
2. Enter the student ID and name in the respective fields.
3. Click on the "Take Image" button to capture the image.
4. The image will be saved in the "TrainingImage" folder.

### Training Students

To train students, follow these steps:

1. Click on the "Train Student" button.
2. The application will train the student using the images taken earlier.
3. The trained data will be saved in the "TrainingImageLabel" folder.

### Marking Attendance

To mark attendance, follow these steps:

1. Click on the "Attendance" button.
2. Select the subject from the dropdown menu.
3. Click on the "Fill Attendance" button to mark attendance.
4. The attendance will be saved in the "Attendance" folder.
